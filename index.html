The Fastest Practical Method

Lang5K is built around sentence-first learning: understand useful full sentences, recall them from English, listen and shadow, then use cloze and dictation to expose weak spots. Grammar is learned from patterns first, then explained where it matters.

1. ListenHear the sentence and pronunciation first.
2. RecallTry to produce Russian from memory before the answer appears.
3. RevealCompare, listen again, and correct the gap immediately.
4. ClozeFill the missing high-value word from context.
5. DictationWrite what you hear to train real listening.
6. ReviewKeep difficult sentences in spaced repetition.
