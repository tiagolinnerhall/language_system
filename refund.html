Refund Policy

Lang5K does not currently run self-serve checkout inside this static site. Paid beta access, if offered, is confirmed manually before payment.

Before taking public payments at scale, Lang5K should publish final checkout terms, refund timing, support response expectations, and cancellation rules on the payment page itself.

For any paid beta issue, contact billing@lang5k.com with your payment details and the problem you experienced.
