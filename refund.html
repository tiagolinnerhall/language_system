Refund Policy

Lang5K paid beta access is sold as a one-time Stripe checkout while the product is still improving. It is not a recurring subscription.

If the product does not work for you, request a refund within 7 days of purchase by emailing billing@lang5k.com. Include the email used at checkout, the Stripe receipt, and the problem you experienced.

Refunds are reviewed against the payment record and beta access status. Abuse, chargeback fraud, scraping, or attempts to copy the service may be refused.
