Privacy Policy

Lang5K stores learning progress and paid-access tokens in your browser using local storage. That includes learned sentences, review-bin items, spaced-review data, study statistics, and the browser token issued after verified checkout.

What We Do Not Collect Here

Lang5K does not store card numbers. Stripe handles checkout and payment details. This version does not include a backend database for learning progress.

Third Parties

The app loads fonts and PDF export libraries from public CDNs. Hosted audio is served from Cloudflare R2 through audio.lang5k.com. Checkout is handled by Stripe. Support email is handled by the email provider.

Your Control

You can clear Lang5K progress by clearing browser site data for this domain.
