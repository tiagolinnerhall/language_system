Privacy Policy

Lang5K stores learning progress in your browser using local storage and, after account sign-in, syncs progress to a server-side account record. That includes learned sentences, review-bin items, spaced-review data, and study statistics. Paid access and account sessions use secure HttpOnly cookies issued after checkout or email-code verification.

What We Do Not Collect Here

Lang5K does not store card numbers. Stripe handles checkout and payment details. Lang5K stores customer entitlement records, account records, progress snapshots, access-recovery events, and operational analytics needed to run the course.

Third Parties

The app loads fonts and PDF export libraries from public CDNs. Hosted audio is served from Cloudflare R2 through audio.lang5k.com. Checkout is handled by Stripe. Login and recovery emails are handled by the email provider. Account/progress data is stored in the configured server database. If you turn on Live Teacher, short audio segments may be sent to the AI provider for transcription and useful transcripts plus lesson context may be sent to the AI teacher. Lang5K does not store those audio segments.

Your Control

You can clear local Lang5K progress by clearing browser site data for this domain. For server-side account/progress questions, email support.
