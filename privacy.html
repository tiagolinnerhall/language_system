Privacy Policy

This static beta version stores learning progress in your browser using local storage. That includes learned sentences, review-bin items, spaced-review data, and study statistics.

What We Do Not Collect Here

This version does not include user accounts, payment forms, analytics scripts, or a backend database for learning progress.

Third Parties

The app loads fonts and PDF export libraries from public CDNs. Hosted audio is served from Cloudflare R2 through audio.lang5k.com. If paid beta onboarding happens by email, that email conversation is handled by the email provider.

Your Control

You can clear Lang5K progress by clearing browser site data for this domain.
